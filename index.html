An Ode to The Central Valley

Things I love about my mission

Jump In Where?

The Central Valley A.K.A The BEST place to grow nearly anything.

Oranges

The entire Central Valley is covered in Orange groves. There is no orange that tastes better than one straight from the trees of Fresno.

Grapes

Fresno is also home to one of the largest vineyards -- owned by the church! It is in this vineyard that many of the raisins used to support and feed those in need are grown.

Almonds

The best thing about the almonds isn't just that almond milk is incredibly more affordable there, but the blossoms on the trees every spring are to die for.

Peaches

Words cannot describe how delicious these peaches are. Modesto California is where I ate the largest and juciest peaches of my life.

"The Central Valley Produce is the best produce on the planet. Once you have tasted of the fruit of the valley, there is no going back"
Everyone on the planet / But mainly Me
